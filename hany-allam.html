Hany Allam

INCO Architects took the challenge of creating an exquisite private residence which Wit and charm balance the life with the new, unlocking the potential of a modern house in Madinaty to create a modern luxury dwelling for a private client.

The House site is located in Madinaty between new cairo and new capital administrative, a short walk from South-Park. The practice reconfigured the existing plans to maximise floor to ceiling heights, open up the living spaces, use natural material and optimise natural light into the home.

The design carefully juxtaposes the clean lines of contemporary interventions, which include a new design for kitchen and minimalistic dining zone, with the distinctive existing features.
